Home Content About
  • Augmented Reality for Everyone.

    • A wearable "mnemonic device”.

    • When we can remember a face, but not the name, or when we arrive at the grocery store and realize we have forgotten our lists, the MneMonocle is our friend.

  • Ingenious.

    • The MneMonocle responds to a person’s eye movements, verbal cues, and gestures. It can sense when we are trying to remember something because it will interpret the direction of our eye glances through what is called neuro-linguistic programming, (NLP).

  • Visionary.

    • The MneMonocle interprets the wearer’s eye movements in combination with the images taken in through its camera and the sounds received through the hearing aid.

  • Your compassionate friend for aging in place.

    • The MneMonocle supports a lifestyle for seniors who want to sustain their independence, and for their families who need extra caregiving support.
